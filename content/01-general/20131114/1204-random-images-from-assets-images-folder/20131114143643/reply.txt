Text: I suggest this workaround:

Upload the images to the content folder of your homepage. Use names like:

> header-banana.jpg
> header-apple.jpg
> header-grapefruit-jpg

    <? $image = $page->find('home')->images()->filterBy('title', '*=', 'header-')->shuffle()->first(); ?>
